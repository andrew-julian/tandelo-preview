Why Tandelo

Not all placements are equal

We built Tandelo around one idea: that native content works best when it's genuinely editorial, on genuinely engaged publications.

📰
Real publications, real readers

Our network consists of established digital publications with organic audiences — not link farms or ghost sites. Your content appears alongside editorial content readers trust.

✅
Editorial review on every article

Every submission goes through our content standards process. This keeps quality high across the network and protects your brand from appearing alongside low-quality content.

🔗
Do-follow links that hold

Articles are published with do-follow links and stay live permanently. No time limits, no removal — and auto-submitted for indexing at the moment of publication.

One business day turnaround

Standard turnaround is one business day from submission. Partners on our volume programs get dedicated editorial queues and priority processing.

Growing publication network

Tandelo represents multiple publications across different audiences and geographies. As the network grows, your content reaches new audiences without changing your workflow.

Partner program for agencies
