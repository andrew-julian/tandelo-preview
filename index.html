Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Tandelo's audience? +

Tandelo's flagship publication, inkl, reaches over 2 million engaged readers globally — premium digital news subscribers who actively seek quality journalism. As the network grows, new publications add targeted audiences across different verticals and geographies.

What content is allowed? +

We accept original, well-written content on a wide range of topics. Some sensitive categories (gambling, crypto, legal, medical) are allowed at a higher rate with enhanced review. We don't accept content promoting harmful products, pure AI output without human editing, or content exploiting fear or tragedy. Read the full standards.

How long do articles stay live? +

Permanently. Once published and paid for, articles remain live with no expiry. They're auto-submitted for indexing at the point of publication.

How can I track performance? +

Articles are published as indexed, do-follow content — you can track them with any standard SEO tool. Partner program clients receive performance summaries and dedicated contacts for reporting queries.

What is the pricing model? +

$75 per article for standard topics, $175 for restricted categories. Optional add-ons: section upgrade (+$40), extra links (+$30), extra words (+$30). Volume agencies can apply for our partner program with rebates up to 8%.

Do you write articles? +

No — we're a placement service, not a content agency. You supply the article; we review and publish it. If you need a writer, we can point you toward freelance options.
