FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to get an article live?

Standard turnaround is one business day from submission, assuming the article meets our content standards. Partner program clients on Silver and Gold tiers get priority queuing and can expect faster turnaround.

How long do articles stay live?

Permanently. Once published and paid for, articles remain live on the publication with no expiry date. They're auto-submitted for indexing at the point of publication.

What if my article is declined?

We'll explain why and suggest revisions where possible. A decline doesn't close the door — it usually just means the article needs a different angle or the link needs to change. Read our standards to understand what we're looking for.

Do you write articles?

No. We're an editorial placement service, not a content writing agency. You supply the article; we review and place it. If you need a writer, there are many freelance content agencies we can recommend.

Can I place articles in a specific section?

Yes — our Section Upgrade add-on (+$40) places your article in a specific content section (e.g. Business, Technology, Health) for more targeted audience reach.

I'm not sure if my article is Category 1 or Category 2 — what do I do?

Use the contact form and tell us your topic and the service/product you're promoting. We'll assess it and confirm the category before you submit. If it changes after full review, we always confirm with you before invoicing.

Do you accept AI-generated content?

Only if it's been meaningfully human-edited. Pure AI output with no editorial revision isn't accepted. We're not looking for proof — but quality articles reflect human judgment, and we can tell the difference.

I'm an agency placing content for multiple clients. Is there a better deal?

Yes — our partner program is designed for exactly this. Bronze starts at 20 articles/year with dedicated support; Gold at 150+ articles/year includes an 8% rebate and a formal partner agreement. Get in touch to apply.
