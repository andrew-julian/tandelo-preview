Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an article live? +

Standard turnaround is one business day from submission, assuming the article meets our content standards. Silver and Gold partner program clients get priority queuing and shorter turnaround.

How long do articles stay live? +

Permanently. Once published and paid for, articles remain live with no expiry. They're auto-submitted for indexing at the point of publication.

What if my article is declined? +

We'll explain why and suggest revisions where possible. A decline doesn't close the door — it usually means the article needs a different angle, the link needs to change, or the framing needs adjustment. Read our standards before submitting.

Do you write articles? +

No. We're a placement service, not a content writing agency. You supply the article; we review and place it.

I'm not sure if my article is Category 1 or 2 — help? +

Use the contact form and tell us your topic and the service you're promoting. We'll confirm the category before you submit. If it changes after full review, we always confirm with you before invoicing.

Can I place in a specific section? +

Yes — our Section Upgrade add-on (+$40) places your article in a specific content section (e.g. Business, Technology, Health) for more targeted reach.

Do you accept AI-generated content? +

Only if it's been meaningfully human-edited. Pure AI output without editorial revision isn't accepted. Quality articles reflect human judgment, and we can tell the difference.

I'm an agency placing for multiple clients. Is there a better deal? +

Yes — our partner program is designed for exactly this. Bronze starts at 20 articles/year with a dedicated contact; Gold at 150+ articles/year includes an 8% rebate and a formal MSA.
