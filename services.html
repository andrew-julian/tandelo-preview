Payment

Payment terms

We invoice via PayPal. Payment is due within 7 days of invoicing — articles go live on payment. We send two reminders; unpaid articles are removed on day 8.

Cancelled invoices can be reissued for a $15 admin fee.

  • Prepayment required for orders of more than 2 articles
  • Prepayment required if you have more than 2 unpaid invoices
  • Prepayment required if any previous invoice is overdue
  • Prepayment required if you have a previous unpaid order
  • First-time submissions are prepaid as standard

Partner payment terms

Partner program clients (Bronze and above) receive Net-15 terms — invoice on placement, payment due 15 days later.

Gold partners can negotiate Net-30 and quarterly consolidated invoicing as part of their MSA.
